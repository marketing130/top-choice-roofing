What We Offer

Residential Metal Roofing Installation

Metal roofing is the premium long-term investment for your home. With a lifespan of 50 years or more, a properly installed metal roof can outlast two or three asphalt roofs — making it the last roof you'll ever need.

At Top Choice Roofing and Siding, we install two primary metal roofing systems: standing seam, with its clean modern lines and concealed fasteners, and metal tile, which replicates the look of traditional clay, slate, or shake at a fraction of the weight. Both systems are engineered to shed snow, resist wind uplift, and perform through decades of freeze-thaw cycles.

Our metal roofing installations include a full assessment of your structure, proper underlayment, ventilation improvements where needed, and flashings sealed to manufacturer specifications. Every project is backed by our written workmanship warranty.

Why Metal Roofing

The Premium Roofing Investment

Metal roofing outperforms every other material across durability, efficiency, and long-term value.

⏱

50+ Year Lifespan

A properly installed metal roof can last the lifetime of your home, outlasting asphalt shingles by 20–30 years.

⚡

Energy Efficient

Metal reflects solar radiation rather than absorbing it, reducing cooling costs in summer by up to 25%.

❄

Sheds Snow Naturally

Metal's smooth surface allows snow and ice to slide off naturally, reducing structural load and ice dam formation.

🔥

Fire Resistant

Metal roofing carries a Class A fire resistance rating — the highest available — providing added protection for your home.

♻

Eco-Friendly

Metal roofing contains recycled content and is 100% recyclable at end of life — a sustainable choice for environmentally conscious homeowners.

🏠

Increases Home Value

A metal roof is a strong selling feature that increases resale value and can lower home insurance premiums.

Metal roofing typically costs 2–3 times more than asphalt shingles upfront.
