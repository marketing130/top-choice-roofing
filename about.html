Who We Are

Your Trusted Roofing & Siding Partner

Top Choice Roofing and Siding has been protecting homes and businesses across the Greater Toronto Area for over 20 years. What started as a small crew of passionate roofers has grown into one of the GTA's most trusted exterior renovation companies.

We specialize in residential and commercial roofing — asphalt shingles, metal, cedar shake, flat, and synthetic slate — as well as a full suite of siding and exterior finishing services.

Our commitment is simple: do the job right, use quality materials, and stand behind our work with strong warranties. Every project gets the same attention to detail, whether it's a single repair or a full commercial reroof.
