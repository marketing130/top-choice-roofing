What We Offer

Quality Asphalt Shingle Installation & Replacement

Asphalt shingles are the most widely used roofing material in Canada for good reason — they strike the perfect balance between performance, affordability, and visual appeal. At Top Choice Roofing and Siding, we install architectural (laminate) and three-tab shingles from industry-leading manufacturers including GAF, Owens Corning, and IKO.

Every installation begins with a thorough inspection of your existing roof deck, ventilation, and drainage. We use quality underlayment, ice-and-water shield at all vulnerable areas, and proper flashing to ensure a watertight, long-lasting result.

Whether you're replacing an aging roof or installing on a new build, our certified team delivers a clean, professional installation — on time and within budget — backed by both manufacturer product warranties and our own workmanship guarantee.

Common Questions

Asphalt Shingle FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear most often about asphalt shingle roofing.

Quality architectural asphalt shingles typically last 25–40 years in Ontario's climate. Three-tab shingles average 15–25 years. Proper installation, ventilation, and routine maintenance are the biggest factors in maximizing the lifespan of your roof.
Three-tab shingles are thinner, lighter, and have a flat, uniform appearance. Architectural (laminate) shingles are thicker, heavier, and have a dimensional, textured look that resembles wood shake or slate. Architectural shingles are more durable and carry longer warranties. We recommend architectural shingles for most residential projects.
The cost depends on your roof's size, pitch, complexity, and the shingle product selected. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate — we'll inspect your roof and provide a detailed written quote with no surprises.
We recommend a full tear-off in most cases. Installing over existing shingles (re-roofing) can trap moisture and hide deck damage, which shortens the life of the new roof and voids many manufacturer warranties. We inspect the deck, replace any damaged sheathing, and start fresh for the best long-term result.
You receive two warranties: a manufacturer product warranty (typically 30–50 years depending on the shingle line) and our workmanship warranty covering the installation itself. We'll walk you through both warranties before your project begins.
Most residential shingle roof replacements are completed in one to two days, depending on roof size and complexity. We clean up completely at the end of each day and leave your property tidy throughout the project.
