Residential Siding Services

We protect and beautify the homes of homeowners across the GTA with a full range of residential siding systems — designed to last, built to perform.

Vinyl Siding

The most popular siding choice for Canadian homeowners. Affordable, low-maintenance, and available in a wide range of colours and profiles with long-lasting durability.

Engineered Wood Siding

The natural beauty of real wood without the high maintenance. Engineered wood siding delivers authentic texture and warmth with superior impact resistance and longevity.

Hardie Board Siding

James Hardie® fibre cement siding — the premium choice for fire resistance, rot resistance, and a 50-year warranty. Engineered specifically for Canadian climates.

Soffit & Fascia
