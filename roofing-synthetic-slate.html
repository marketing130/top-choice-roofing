What We Offer

Synthetic Slate Roofing Installation

Natural slate is one of the most beautiful roofing materials in existence — but its weight, fragility, and cost put it out of reach for most homeowners. Synthetic slate solves all three problems without sacrificing the sophisticated look that makes slate so desirable.

Made from engineered polymer composites and recycled materials, synthetic slate tiles are lightweight, impact-resistant, and available in a wide range of colours and profiles that authentically replicate the texture and variation of natural stone. Most products carry a Class 4 impact resistance rating — the highest available — along with a Class A fire rating.

At Top Choice Roofing and Siding, we install premium synthetic slate systems from trusted manufacturers, with lifespans of 40–50 years. Every installation includes a full structural assessment to ensure your roof deck is suited to the new system, along with proper flashing and ventilation details for long-term performance.
