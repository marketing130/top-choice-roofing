Complete Commercial Roofing for the GTA

Commercial roofing demands a different level of expertise, planning, and execution than residential work. Larger surface areas, complex drainage systems, rooftop equipment penetrations, and the need to minimize business disruption all require a contractor with the right experience and systems.

Top Choice Roofing has completed commercial projects for retail plazas, office buildings, warehouses, multi-unit residential buildings, and industrial facilities across Toronto and the surrounding region. We work with the full range of commercial roofing systems — TPO and EPDM flat membranes, standing seam and exposed fastener metal, and commercial-grade asphalt shingles for low-slope applications.

Every commercial project begins with a detailed site assessment, load calculations, and a written scope of work. We coordinate with property managers, building owners, and tenants to schedule work with minimal disruption, and all our commercial work meets Ontario Building Code requirements and manufacturer specifications.

Commercial Roofing Systems We Install

We offer the right system for every commercial application — from low-slope flat roofs to steep-slope metal and shingle systems.

🏢

TPO Flat Roofing

Thermoplastic polyolefin (TPO) is the leading choice for commercial flat roofs — energy efficient, UV-resistant, and highly durable in Canadian climates. Ideal for retail, office, and warehouse buildings.

🔩

EPDM Membrane

Ethylene propylene diene monomer (EPDM) offers outstanding flexibility and weather resistance, particularly in extreme cold. A proven system for low-slope commercial applications with 20+ year lifespans.

⚡

Modified Bitumen

Two-ply modified bitumen systems offer excellent puncture resistance and redundancy. A reliable choice for high-traffic flat roofs and buildings with rooftop HVAC equipment.

🏭

Commercial Metal Roofing

Standing seam and R-panel metal roofing for warehouses, industrial facilities, and commercial buildings. Exceptionally durable, low-maintenance, and available in a wide range of profiles and finishes.

🏘️

Multi-Unit Residential

Complete roofing solutions for townhome complexes, condominiums, and multi-unit residential buildings. We work efficiently on large-scale projects with coordinated scheduling and site management.

🔧

Commercial Roof Repair

Emergency and planned repairs for all commercial roofing systems. We respond quickly to leaks and damage to minimize business interruption, and provide detailed condition reports for maintenance planning.
