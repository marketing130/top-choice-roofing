What We Offer

Quality Vinyl Siding Installation & Replacement

Vinyl siding is the most widely installed siding material in Canada for good reason — it delivers outstanding value, durability, and low maintenance demands. At Top Choice Roofing and Siding, we install premium vinyl siding products from industry-leading manufacturers across the Greater Toronto Area.

Every installation begins with a thorough assessment of your existing exterior, moisture barriers, and insulation. We prepare the wall surface properly before installing quality house wrap and locking panels into place for a tight, weather-resistant finish.

Whether you're replacing aging siding, upgrading your home's curb appeal, or adding insulated vinyl for improved energy efficiency, our certified team delivers a clean, professional result — on time and within budget.

Why Choose Vinyl Siding

Built for Canadian Homes & Climates

Vinyl siding offers exceptional value for homeowners — here's why it remains the top siding choice across the GTA.

💰

Affordable

Vinyl siding offers the best value of any siding material — lower installed cost without sacrificing durability or visual appeal.

✅

Low Maintenance

Unlike wood, vinyl never needs painting, staining, or sealing. An occasional rinse with a garden hose keeps it looking like new.

🌟

20–40 Year Lifespan

Quality vinyl siding is engineered to last 20–40 years, providing decades of reliable protection with minimal upkeep required.

❄

Weather Resistant

Designed to handle Canadian winters, heavy rain, UV exposure, and high winds without fading, cracking, or warping.

🎨

Wide Colour Selection

Available in dozens of colours and profiles — from traditional clapboard to dutch lap and vertical — to suit any home style.

🏠

Insulated Options

Insulated vinyl siding adds a continuous layer of rigid foam behind the panels, improving energy efficiency and reducing heating costs.

Common Questions

Vinyl Siding FAQs

Answers to the questions we hear most often about vinyl siding.

Quality vinyl siding typically lasts 20–40 years in Ontario's climate. The lifespan depends on the thickness and grade of the vinyl, the quality of installation, and basic maintenance. Thicker premium-grade panels generally outlast economy options significantly.
No — vinyl siding is manufactured with colour built into the material, so it never needs painting or staining. The colour won't peel, chip, or flake. Over many years some fading may occur, but premium vinyl panels are UV-stabilized to retain colour much longer.
Insulated vinyl siding has a layer of rigid foam insulation moulded to the back of each panel. This adds thermal performance, reduces outside noise, and makes the panels more rigid and dent-resistant. For GTA homeowners looking to improve energy efficiency, insulated vinyl is a worthwhile upgrade.
In some cases, yes — vinyl can be installed over existing wood siding if it's structurally sound and flat. However, we typically recommend removing the old siding so we can inspect the sheathing and moisture barrier underneath, correct any damage, and ensure a proper, long-lasting installation.
