Toronto's Trusted Roof Repair Specialists

A damaged roof can't wait. Water infiltration, missing shingles, or storm damage can escalate within hours — causing structural damage, mould growth, and costly interior repairs. At Top Choice Roofing, we respond fast and fix it right the first time.

Our certified technicians are trained to diagnose roof problems quickly and accurately. Whether you're dealing with a small leak around a chimney flashing or extensive damage after a severe storm, we have the expertise and equipment to handle repairs on every roofing system — asphalt, metal, cedar, flat, and more.

We don't believe in band-aid solutions. Every repair comes with a thorough inspection so you understand the full condition of your roof and can make informed decisions. Our work is backed by a workmanship warranty so you can have confidence in every repair we complete.

Roof Repair FAQs

Common questions about our repair and emergency services.

For genuine emergencies — active leaks, storm blow-offs, or structural damage — we aim to have a technician on-site within hours. We offer 24/7 emergency service and will provide temporary weatherproofing (tarping) to protect your home until a permanent repair can be completed.

This depends on the age of your roof, the extent of the damage, and the overall condition of the existing material. If your roof is under 15 years old and damage is localized, repair is almost always the right call. If it's older with widespread wear, a replacement may be more cost-effective long term. Our technicians will give you an honest assessment with no pressure to upsell.

We can perform emergency tarping and temporary repairs year-round. For permanent repairs, some materials (like asphalt shingles) require temperatures above 5°C to seal properly. Where conditions allow, we'll complete the permanent repair as soon as safely possible. We'll always be transparent about timing and what can be done immediately.

Yes. We have extensive experience working alongside homeowners and insurance adjusters for storm, hail, and wind damage claims. We'll provide detailed documentation of all damage found, itemized repair costs, and photographs to support your claim. We can also speak with your adjuster directly if needed.

We repair all common types of roof damage including: missing or damaged shingles, leaks and water infiltration, storm and hail damage, flashing failures (around chimneys, skylights, vents), ice dam damage, flat roof membrane punctures or blisters, and fascia or soffit damage. If you're not sure what's wrong, we'll find it during our inspection.

Minor repairs — replacing a few shingles, resealing flashing, patching a flat roof — can typically be completed in a few hours. Larger repairs involving damaged decking or extensive shingle replacement may take a full day or more. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your free estimate so you can plan accordingly.
